## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG, JPG, or WebP recommended
- **Size**: Approximately 200x300 pixels (3:4 aspect ratio)
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display

## Fallback

If card images are not found, the app will automatically fall back to SVG-generated card representations that mimic traditional Spanish card designs.

## Sources

You can find authentic Spanish-suited card images from:
- Traditional Spanish card decks
- Online card image repositories
- Photography of physical Spanish cards
- Digital recreations of traditional designs

Make sure the images show proper Spanish suit symbols (coins, cups, swords, clubs) rather than French suits (diamonds, hearts, spades, clubs).
